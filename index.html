About Us

Trusted Manufacturer
Since 1997

FORGEFLOW METAL INDUSTRIES LLP stands as a leading Manufacturer,supplier & stockist of Flanges, Pipes and Pipe Fittings in Mumbai, India. Our comprehensive range is meticulously crafted for piping applications across steel factories, chemical industries, petrochemical plants, fire protection systems, and more.

With diverse materials including Monel, Inconel, Carbon Steel, Copper Nickel, Duplex and Stainless Steel, our products conform strictly to International Quality Standards (IQS). We also offer fully customized solutions tailored to your requirements.

We proudly serve approximately 10+ countries, dispatching around 15+ containers every month to ports worldwide.

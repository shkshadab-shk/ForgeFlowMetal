Plate Flanges

What is a Plate Flange

About Plate Flanges

A plate flange serves as a flat, circular component featuring evenly spaced bolt holes, employed to connect sections of pipe or equipment together seamlessly. Usually constructed from carbon or stainless steel, it can be welded or screwed onto the end of a pipe or fitting. Widely utilized in industrial settings like oil and gas pipelines, water treatment facilities, and chemical plants, plate flanges ensure a dependable and robust connection, vital for withstanding high pressure and temperature conditions. Available in diverse sizes, pressure ratings, and materials, plate flanges are adaptable to various requirements and applications.

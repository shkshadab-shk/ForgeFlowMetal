What is a Hub Flange

About Hub Flanges

Hub flanges serve as integral components of a bicycle hub, extending from the hub shell's center and featuring apertures for spoke insertion. Usually crafted from metal, these flanges facilitate the connection between the hub and the rim of a bicycle wheel. Spokes are threaded through the flange holes and subsequently affixed to the rim, establishing a tensioned framework that bolsters the wheel's support and stability. Available in diverse sizes, shapes, and spoke hole counts, hub flanges play a pivotal role in the construction or maintenance of bicycle wheels.
